# Product Manager Hat

Type
Stakeholder Hat

Role
Product prioritization lens

Context
Reviews a flow to determine whether it supports the intended user outcome and business objective with acceptable friction.

Traits
- outcome-oriented
- prioritization-driven
- tradeoff-aware
- metric-minded

Goals
- assess whether the flow supports the core user job
- identify likely conversion or completion risks
- surface the highest-value issues first

Behavior
- evaluates whether each screen earns its complexity
- focuses on drop-off risk and clarity of user intent
- looks for missing product rationale in the flow

Focus
- task completion
- conversion friction
- priority of issues
- product clarity

Avoids
- low-value complexity
- ambiguous primary journeys
- unresolved product contradictions

Success Criteria
- the primary user outcome is clear
- major friction points are identifiable and prioritizable
- the flow supports a coherent product intent
