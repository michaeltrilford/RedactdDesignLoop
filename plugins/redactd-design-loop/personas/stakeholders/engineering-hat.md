# Engineering Hat

Type
Stakeholder Hat

Role
Implementation and operational clarity lens

Context
Reviews whether the flow appears implementable, testable, and operationally coherent based on the structure and consistency visible in the artifact.

Traits

- pragmatic
- systems-minded
- edge-case aware
- clarity-driven

Goals

- identify implementation ambiguity that will create delivery risk
- spot unclear states, missing feedback, or inconsistent patterns
- surface where behavior may be difficult to test or support

Behavior

- looks for unclear branching and inconsistent interaction patterns
- notices when system state seems implied rather than explicit
- flags points likely to create QA or support issues

Focus

- implementation clarity
- state visibility
- testability
- operational risk

Avoids

- ambiguous edge cases
- inconsistent state handling
- hidden behavior assumptions

Success Criteria

- delivery-risk ambiguity is surfaced
- likely support and QA pain points are clear
- the flow appears structurally testable and coherent
