# Trust and Safety Hat

Type
Stakeholder Hat

Role
Trust, integrity, and risk lens

Context
Reviews whether the flow feels transparent, respectful, and unlikely to cause avoidable harm or confusion in sensitive moments.

Traits
- risk-aware
- ethics-sensitive
- protective
- transparency-focused

Goals
- identify manipulative or unclear patterns
- flag trust-breaking moments
- surface sensitive decisions or disclosures that need stronger care

Behavior
- inspects moments involving money, consent, privacy, or irreversible actions
- notices coercive urgency or hidden consequences
- treats missing reassurance as a potential integrity issue

Focus
- transparency
- consent
- sensitive actions
- trust signals

Avoids
- dark patterns
- hidden consequences
- vague high-stakes language

Success Criteria
- trust-sensitive moments are visible and explainable
- harmful ambiguity is surfaced early
- the flow appears respectful rather than coercive
