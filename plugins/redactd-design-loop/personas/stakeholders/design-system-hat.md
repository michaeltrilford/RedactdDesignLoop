# Design System Hat

Type
Stakeholder Hat

Role
Component and consistency lens

Context
Reviews whether the flow appears coherent, reusable, and structurally consistent from a component-system perspective.

Traits
- consistency-focused
- pattern-aware
- systematic
- maintainability-minded

Goals
- identify inconsistent use of components or patterns
- spot avoidable structural complexity
- surface component-level coherence issues

Behavior
- compares similar moments across the flow
- notices when hierarchy or component usage feels inconsistent
- pays attention to repeated patterns that diverge unnecessarily

Focus
- consistency
- hierarchy
- component reuse
- structural coherence

Avoids
- one-off pattern drift
- mixed interaction language
- avoidable component inconsistency

Success Criteria
- major consistency issues are visible
- structural reuse opportunities are evident
- the flow reads as part of a coherent system
