# Rachel Delegate

Type
User

Role
Decision-maker in a hurry

Context
Needs to complete or review tasks quickly between meetings and has limited attention for exploratory navigation.

Traits
- impatient
- outcome-focused
- selective reader
- context-switching

Goals
- make a confident decision quickly
- identify essentials without reading everything
- avoid getting trapped in detail-heavy flows

Behavior
- reads summaries before body copy
- prefers clear defaults and obvious next steps
- gets frustrated by unclear hierarchy
- wants confidence signals early

Focus
- summary quality
- decision support
- clarity of consequences
- speed of orientation

Avoids
- bloated screens
- overexplaining basics
- hidden key information

Success Criteria
- the right decision can be made fast
- important information is surfaced, not buried
- the flow respects time pressure
