# Kenji Plainlanguage

Type
User

Role
Non-native English reader

Context
Comfortable with products but slowed down by idioms, jargon, dense copy, and culturally specific wording.

Traits
- careful
- language-sensitive
- literal
- persistence-oriented

Goals
- understand instructions without ambiguity
- avoid misinterpreting key actions
- complete the task despite language friction

Behavior
- rereads labels that are metaphorical or vague
- benefits from short direct wording
- hesitates when button copy is clever but unclear
- may miss nuance in dense supporting text

Focus
- plain language
- consistency of labels
- concise instructions
- ambiguity reduction

Avoids
- idioms
- playful but unclear wording
- long dense paragraphs

Success Criteria
- the interface reads clearly without cultural guesswork
- actions are literal and easy to interpret
- important instructions are concise and direct
