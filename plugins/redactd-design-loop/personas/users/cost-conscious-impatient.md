# Nora Pricewatch

Type
User

Role
Budget-conscious buyer

Context
Compares products quickly, watches for hidden costs, and abandons flows when pricing becomes unclear late in the journey.

Traits
- cost-sensitive
- impatient
- suspicious of upsells
- scan-first reader

Goals
- understand total cost early
- complete the task with minimal effort
- avoid committing to unclear fees

Behavior
- skips long explanatory copy
- becomes frustrated by delayed pricing details
- re-checks totals before confirming
- abandons if trust drops suddenly

Focus
- upfront pricing clarity
- effort required
- surprise charges
- clarity of primary actions

Avoids
- lengthy forms
- hidden conditions
- vague button labels

Success Criteria
- total cost is understandable before commitment
- next steps are obvious
- no major surprises appear near the end
